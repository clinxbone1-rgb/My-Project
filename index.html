I Love you♥

Today, on Valentine’s Day, I’m writing this not because I want to stay, but because I need you to know how deeply I’ve loved you.

This isn’t easy for you after saying goodbye on a day meant for love feels like a contradiction. But I’ve come to realize that love isn’t just about staying together. Sometimes, love means letting go with grace.

I’ll never forget the way you made me feel—seen, cherished, alive. The laughter, the quiet moments, the way your presence made everything brighter. I’m so grateful for every memory, every word, every shared dream.

But I also know that staying would mean holding onto something that no longer serves either of us. I don’t want to leave you with regret or unanswered questions.

I hope you find someone who matches your light, who makes you smile the way you used to make me smile. And I hope, one day, you can look back and say, “That was real.” It was.

With all my love, Yours, always Messiah
